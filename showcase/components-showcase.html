Role Badges

Used on user profile cards and admin panels. Solid dark = elevated permissions (Full Control, Partial Control). Light filled = labeled role (Admin). Outlined = standard role (Officer).

Step Progress Tracker

Horizontal tracker for document request status. Check icons = done. Orange circle = current step. Gray = pending. Add .is-done or .is-active to .step-progress__item. The last item never gets a connector line.

Active at step 3 of 4

Submitted
Under Review
In Progress
Completed

Fully completed

Submitted
Under Review
Ready
Completed
.is-done .is-active (pending, no class)

  <div class="step-progress__item is-done">
    <div class="step-progress__circle">
      <i data-lucide="check"></i>
    </div>

  </div>

  <!-- current / active step -->

Numbered Stepper

Multi-step form flow. Uses numbered circles — not check icons. Used on registration wizard and document request forms. Done steps show a checkmark icon. Active step gets a green ring. Pending = gray.
